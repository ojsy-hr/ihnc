About

What Is This?

ihavenocards is a quick, lightweight web app that helps you play classic darts and pool games without needing a physical deck of cards. It's perfect for pubs, friends' houses, or anywhere else you forgot your cards.

How It Works

Just pick your game from the homepage. You'll be guided through drawing cards, tracking lives, and revealing results when the game ends. All visual, no setup, no hassle — and it works on any device.

Who Made It?

This project was built by a developer who loves games, pubs, and solving real-life annoyances with simple tools. It's a passion project — designed to be fast, free, and fun to use.

Because nobody wants to dig through a drawer looking for cards when you're already halfway through your first pint.
