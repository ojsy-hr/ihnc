About ihavenocards

Free card games, darts scorers & pub games — no download, no sign-up, works on any device.

What is this?

ihavenocards is a lightweight web app for those moments when the cards are nowhere to be found but the game must go on. Whether you're at the pub, at a mate's house, or camping — open the site on any device and you're ready to play.

No app to download. No account to create. Just games, instantly.

🏆 Game Night

Playing multiple games in one evening? Game Night mode ties everything together. Enter your group's names once, then play any game as normal — wins are tracked automatically across every game you play. A live leaderboard shows who's ahead, and at the end of the night you get a full summary of the results.

A floating pill on every game page keeps the current leader visible at a glance, so the bragging rights are always up to date.

How it works

Pick a game from the home page, set up your players, and go. Everything runs in your browser — game state is saved so you can refresh without losing progress, and the site works offline after your first visit.

For a full evening of games, start a Game Night session first. Your player names carry through to every game automatically, and wins are added to the leaderboard the moment a game finishes.

The story

Built by a developer who loves games, pubs, and solving real-life annoyances with simple tools. Because nobody wants to dig through a drawer looking for cards when you're already halfway through your first pint.

ihavenocards is free, open, and always growing. Got feedback or a game idea? We'd love to hear it.
