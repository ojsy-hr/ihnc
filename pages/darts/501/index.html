How to Play

The Game

Each player starts at the chosen score (default 501) and counts down to exactly zero. Enter your turn's total score and hit Submit after each round of 3 darts.

Bust

If your score would go below zero, or you'd land on 1 (which can't be finished with a double), your turn is a bust — your score stays the same and play passes to the next player.

Double Out

When the Double Out option is on, you must finish the game by hitting a double (or the bullseye) as your last dart. If you reach zero without a valid double finish, it's a bust.

Checkout Hints

When your remaining score is 170 or below, a suggested checkout route will appear below the input.
