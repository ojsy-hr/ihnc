How to Play

Setup

Each player picks a unique number (1–20) as their own. Players take turns throwing 3 darts per turn.

Phase 1 — Qualifying

  • Hit your own number 3 times (with any combination of singles, doubles, and triples) to become a Killer
  • Until you qualify, you can't eliminate others

Phase 2 — Killing

  • Once a Killer, hitting another player's number takes a life from them
  • Each player starts with 3 lives
  • 0 lives = eliminated

Winning

Last player with at least 1 life wins!
