Cricket

How to Play

The Game

Players take turns throwing 3 darts. The targets are 20, 19, 18, 17, 16, 15 and Bull. Close all numbers and have the highest score to win.

Closing Numbers

  • Hit a number 3 times to close it
  • A Single = 1 mark, Double = 2 marks, Triple = 3 marks
  • Bull: outer ring = Single (25 pts), inner ring = Double (50 pts)

Scoring

  • Once you close a number, extra hits score points until all opponents close it too
  • Points are the face value of the number times the multiplier

Playing

  • Select Single / Double / Triple then tap the number you hit
  • Miss wastes a dart, Undo takes back your last dart
  • After 3 darts, pass to the next player
