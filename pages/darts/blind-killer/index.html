Blind Killer

How to Play

The Game

Each player draws a card from the deck — but keeps it secret. Every card starts with 3 lives. The last card alive wins.

Setup

  • Set the card range (how many cards in the deck)
  • Set the number of players
  • Each player taps Draw Card to get their secret card (it disappears after 3 seconds)

Playing

  • Once all cards are drawn, tap Reveal All Cards
  • When a player's number is hit on the dartboard, tap their card to take a life
  • A card with 0 lives is eliminated
  • Last card standing wins!
