How It Works

Why This Exists

Have you ever been at a friend's house, at a party, or even at the pub and found yourself with a dartboard, a pool table, and games you want to play — but no cards to hand?

Well, this is exactly why we're here: to make sure you never miss out on the fun, even when you don't have a deck of cards nearby.

Simple to Use

Just pick your game from the homepage. You’ll be guided through drawing cards, tracking lives, and revealing results when the game ends. All visual, no setup, no hassle.

Works on Any Device

Whether you're on your phone, tablet, or laptop — ihavenocards is built to be responsive and easy to use anywhere.
