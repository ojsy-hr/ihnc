How to Play

Setup

The 52-card deck is split equally between two players (26 each). A cover screen hides the cards when passing the phone.

Playing

  • On your turn, tap Flip Card to turn over your top card into the centre pile
  • Pass the phone to the other player

Snap!

  • When the flipped card matches the previous card (same rank), both players race to tap SNAP!
  • First to tap SNAP wins all the cards in the centre pile

Winning

The player who runs out of cards loses. The player with all the cards wins.
