How to Play

Goal

Beat the Banker by getting closer to 21 without busting. A Pontoon (Ace + 10-value card) or Five Card Trick beats everything.

Card Values

  • 2–10 = face value, J/Q/K = 10, Ace = 1 or 11

Your Options

  • Twist — take a card face up (always free)
  • Stick — hold your hand (must have 15+)
  • Buy — take a card face down (only before twisting, max 5 cards)

Special Hands

  • Pontoon — Ace + 10-value card on first two cards (best hand)
  • Five Card Trick — 5 cards totalling 21 or less (second best)

Banker Rules

  • Banker must keep hitting until reaching 17 or higher
  • On a tie, the Banker wins
