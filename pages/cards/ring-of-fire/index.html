Ring of Fire

The Rules

A — Waterfall 🌊

Everyone drinks. You can only stop when the person to your right stops.

2 — You 👉

Choose someone to take a drink.

3 — Me 🤙

You drink.

4 — Floor 🫳

Last person to touch the floor drinks.

5 — Guys 🍺

All guys drink.

6 — Chicks 🍷

All girls drink.

7 — Heaven ☝️

Last to point to the sky drinks.

8 — Mate 🤝

Pick a drinking buddy — they drink whenever you do until a new 8 is drawn.

9 — Rhyme 🎤

Say a word. Go around rhyming. First to fail or repeat drinks.

10 — Categories 📋

Name a category (e.g. "football clubs"). Go around — first to fail drinks.

J — Make a Rule 📜

Create a rule that applies for the rest of the game. Break it and drink.

Q — Question Master ❓

You're the Question Master until the next Queen. Anyone who answers your question drinks.

K — King's Cup 👑

Pour some of your drink into the King's Cup (a glass in the middle). The person who draws the 4th King must drink the whole cup!
