How to Play

Four Rounds

Each player tries to answer all 4 questions correctly. Get one wrong — take a sip and start over from Round 1!

Round 1 — Red or Black?

Guess the colour (suit) of the next card.

Round 2 — Higher or Lower?

Guess if the next card is higher or lower than Round 1's card. (Same = Lower loses)

Round 3 — Inside or Outside?

Guess if the next card's value falls between (Inside) or outside the values of your first two cards.

Round 4 — What Suit?

Guess the exact suit (♠ ♥ ♦ ♣) of the next card.
