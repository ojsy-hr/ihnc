Blackjack

How to Play

Goal

Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over ("busting").

Card Values

  • 2–10 = face value
  • J, Q, K = 10
  • Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever is better)

Playing

  • Hit — take another card
  • Stand — keep your current hand
  • Double — take exactly one more card
  • If you go over 21, you bust and lose

Dealer Rules

  • Dealer must hit until they reach 17 or higher
  • Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card) beats everything except another Blackjack
