Cutthroat Pool 🎱

Three players. Balls 1–5, 6–10, and 11–15 are assigned by card draw (or choose below). Pot your opponents' balls — last group on the table wins.

Tap a ball to mark it as potted

🏆

Cutthroat Pool

How to Play

Setup

  • 3 players, each assigned a group of 5 balls (1–5, 6–10, or 11–15)
  • Groups are assigned randomly or by choice

Gameplay

  • Players take turns at the pool table as normal
  • Aim to pot your opponents' balls, not your own
  • When a ball is potted, tap it in the app to mark it

Winning

  • A player is eliminated when all 5 of their balls are potted
  • The last player with at least one ball still on the table wins

Note

If you pot the 8-ball, you're out — follow your local house rules for scratches.
